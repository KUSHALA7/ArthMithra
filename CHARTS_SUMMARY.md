# 📊 CHARTS & VISUALIZATIONS - COMPLETE!

## ✅ What's Been Added

### 1. **Risk Gauge Visualization** ⚠️
- **Interactive SVG gauge** showing Financial Risk Score (0-100)
- **Color-coded levels**: 🟢 Low | 🟡 Medium | 🔴 High
- **Risk breakdown** showing individual factors:
  - Emergency Fund status
  - Term Insurance coverage
  - Health Insurance adequacy
  - Debt Ratio analysis

**Impact**: Visually compelling risk assessment judges will love!

### 2. **Net Worth Projection Graph** 📈
- **12-month forward projection** line chart
- Shows net worth growth based on monthly surplus
- Interactive tooltips with value formatting
- Smooth animations and gradients
- Uses Chart.js for professional rendering

**Key Metrics Shown**:
- Current net worth
- Projected growth trajectory
- Monthly increase rate

### 3. **Expense Breakdown Pie Chart** 💰
- **Doughnut chart** showing expense distribution
- Categories: Rent, Food, Transport, Insurance, Entertainment, Utilities
- Percentage breakdown with actual values
- Color-coded segments for easy identification
- Legend at bottom with hover interactions

**Key Insights**:
- Identifies spending patterns
- Highlights major expense categories
- Shows percentage allocation

### 4. **Asset Allocation Chart** 💎
- Horizontal bar chart showing asset distribution
- Categories: Emergency Fund, MF, Stocks, FDs, Gold, Crypto
- Easy comparison of portfolio composition
- Built-in but ready to use anywhere

### 5. **SIP Projection Chart** 📊
- Stacked bar chart showing SIP growth
- Principal vs Returns visualization
- 5-year projection with monthly breakdowns
- Shows power of compounding
